Welcome!

I post algorithms and USACO solutions on my website. I break up each post into both blocks of code and blocks of text. Text is written like this. Code is written as it is below:

int x = 2;

This makes it easy to read and interpret. code that I post. At the end of every post, I will give you resources to further expand your knowledge. For USACO posts, I will give links to websites, YouTube videos, books, or other medias that specifically talk about algorithms I used.

Note that my solutions, while unique, are not the only answer! I encourage you to solve the problem on your own, in your own way. My algorithms are based on my thinking processes -- yours will most likely be different!

Apart from that, welcome to my website!
